Vision

Through proactive visualisation and actualisation of policy reform and by working in tandem with and against existing environmental policies. Niti Manthan CELRA strives to promote the unity of national and regional policy making.

Mission

  • To ensure that the general public has easy access to environmental policy and associated resources.

  • To bridge the gap between academia and policy implementation at grassroots levels, by creating opportunities for visible change outside legal research and academic progress.

  • To shift the focus to actionable change from research oriented discourse.

  • To expand the scope of inclusivity in environmental policy making and include student opinion.
